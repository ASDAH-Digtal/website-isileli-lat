Circuits

What is a circuit?


A circuit is an electrical component that powers all technology and devices. Often, a circuit consists
of a power source (commonly a battery or cell), wires to transport the electrical current, and the main electrical
component which is being powered. This is commonly a bulb, a buzzer, an LED light, or any other electrical component.

Components that circuits often contain:

  • A power source
  • A conducting wire
  • A component needing to be powered
  • A resistor decreasing current
  • An LED light
