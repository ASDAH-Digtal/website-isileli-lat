Circuits
A circuit is an electrical component that powers all technology and devices. Often, a circuit consists of a power source (commonly a battery or cell), wires to transport the electrical current, and the main electrical component which is being powered. This is commonly a bulb, a buzzer, an LED light, or any other
Circuits often contain: - A power source - A conducting wire - A component needing to be powered.
